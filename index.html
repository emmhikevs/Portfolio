Overview

A crypto trading and portfolio management app designed for both novice investors and power traders. The primary challenge was presenting real-time, volatile financial data in a way that is readable and immediately actionable — without overwhelming users with information density.

Problem

The existing app had a cluttered interface with no clear information hierarchy, causing users to abandon transactions mid-flow. Task completion sat at 61% — well below the industry benchmark of 78%. User interviews revealed confusion around portfolio performance metrics and a fear of making errors during trades.

Solution

Redesigned the information architecture from scratch, introduced a modular card system for portfolio data, and reduced the transaction flow from 7 steps to 3. A comprehensive dark-mode design system was built to reduce eye strain during extended trading sessions. After launch, task completion improved from 61% to 89% and session duration rose 34%.

Overview

A fashion e-commerce app targeting millennial shoppers who expect a premium, frictionless mobile experience comparable to Zara or ASOS. The client needed to close the gap between browsing and buying.

Problem

Cart abandonment sat at 72%, driven by a complex multi-page checkout and poor product discovery. Mobile-first users were dropping off at the payment screen due to excessive form fields and no guest checkout option. Product filter UX was also confusing, leading to low engagement with the full catalogue.

Solution

Introduced a single-screen checkout with Apple Pay/Google Pay integration, rebuilt product filters with live preview, and added a wishlist-to-cart gesture shortcut. Abandonment dropped from 72% to 43% (a 40% reduction) while checkout conversion rose 28%.

Overview

High-converting landing page for an AI productivity SaaS targeting knowledge workers. The brief required communicating complex AI capabilities quickly to a sceptical, technically literate audience who had seen many similar tools.

Problem

The previous landing page had a 2.3% trial conversion rate. Messaging was too technical, CTAs were buried below the fold, and the page lacked social proof in the first viewport — causing most users to bounce within 10 seconds of arrival.

Solution

Rebuilt around a benefit-first narrative. Moved social proof and outcome metrics into the hero. Introduced a single clear above-the-fold CTA and scroll-triggered animations that guide users through the value proposition step by step. Trial conversion improved from 2.3% to 4.1% — a 78% uplift.

Overview

A professional trading dashboard for a crypto exchange handling $2M+ in daily volume. Target users were experienced traders who needed high information density without sacrificing readability under the pressure of live markets.

Problem

The old dashboard displayed 14 panels by default with no customisation options. New users churned within 48 hours due to cognitive overload, while power users were frustrated by inability to rearrange their workspace. Support tickets were high due to poor onboarding UX.

Solution

Designed a modular drag-and-drop panel system with sensible defaults for beginner vs. advanced user modes. Introduced a dark-mode-first design system with colour-coded data states (green/red/amber) and a persistent watchlist. 48h churn dropped 37%, daily active users grew 41%, and support tickets fell 30%.
